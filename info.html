What is a Bar Mitzvah?

A Bar Mitzvah is a ceremony for a Jewish boy turning 13. It commemorates the boy becoming an adult and reading the Torah for the first time in front of the congregation.

What do I wear?

For the service: Nice clothing/same as church attire (No jeans)
For the party: Cocktail attire, Sneakers.
