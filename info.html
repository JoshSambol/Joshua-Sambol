What is a barmitzvah?

A Barmitzvah is ceremony for a Jewish boy turning 13. It comemorates the boy becoming an adult and reading the torah for the first time in front of the congragation.

What do I wear?

For the service: Nice clothing/same as church attire(No jeans)
For the party: Cocktail attire, Sneakers.
